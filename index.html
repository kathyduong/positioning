About Me

Hi, my name is Kathy and while I wouldn't consider myself a designer; I do have over 1200 hours on Animal Crossing: New Horizons! Considering I was creating custom Tumblr themes with HTML in my youth, it only makes sense that I am now an aspiring UI/UX designer. In all seriousness, I am currently attending an accelerated program called Year Up in the Greater Seattle area where I study a diverse set of programming languages like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Before that, I completed my Associate's Degree summa cum laude at Bellevue College and worked in various customer service and supervising roles. When my time isn't consumed by education, I enjoy snuggling up with my pets and occasionally cringe at websites using comic sans.
